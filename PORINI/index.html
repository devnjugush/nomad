About Porini Nomads

Our Story

Founded in 2025 by a group of passionate outdoor enthusiasts, Porini nomads Adventures has been creating unforgettable camping experiences. What started as a small group leading weekend hikes has grown into a premier adventure company serving many happy campers.

Our mission is simple: to connect people with nature through authentic, sustainable, and transformative outdoor experiences. We believe in responsible tourism that preserves our natural spaces while creating meaningful adventures for our guests.

Our Team

Our guides are certified wilderness experts with extensive knowledge of local ecosystems, survival skills, and first aid. Our team ensures your safety and enjoyment throughout the journey.

Our Values

  • Sustainability: Minimizing our environmental impact
  • Safety: Your well-being is our top priority
  • Authenticity: Real wilderness experiences
  • Community: Building connections with nature and people

Frequently Asked Questions

What should I pack for my camping tour?

We provide a comprehensive packing list specific to each tour 30 days before departure. Generally, you'll need comfortable hiking shoes, weather-appropriate clothing, a reusable water bottle, personal toiletries, and any medications. We provide all camping gear including tents, sleeping bags, and cooking equipment.

What is your cancellation policy?

We understand plans can change. For cancellations made more than 60 days before departure, we offer a full refund. Between 30-60 days, we offer a 50% refund. Within 30 days, we offer a 25% refund or the option to reschedule. We strongly recommend travel insurance to cover unexpected circumstances.

What level of fitness is required?

Each tour has a different difficulty rating (easy, moderate, challenging) which is clearly indicated in the tour description. Our easiest tours require only basic mobility while our challenging tours require previous hiking experience and good cardiovascular fitness. If you have concerns about your fitness level, please contact us before booking.

Are your tours suitable for children?

We offer family-friendly tours specifically designed for children ages 8 and up. These tours have shorter hiking distances, more breaks, and engaging activities for younger participants. Children must be accompanied by an adult. Please check the tour description for age recommendations.
